
Serenity is a **Third Party Store** for iOS allowing you to install .ipa's outside of the App Store, although there are other Third Party Stores such as FlekSt0re, Feather and KSign **this is what makes us stand out**:

- Simple and easy to use (along with the tutorials)
- Be Updated with the Sideloading Community by our News
- Best Certificate Management (Analyse, Export, Import)
- Signing repository .ipa's using another signer installed on your device
- Upcoming Features nobody will expect
- Certificate Giveaways

Serenity isn't exactly an app, its a Webapp which means that **the app is just a website**, it is done with [https://framework7.io](https://framework7.io)
